About A R Hospital

Your Trusted Multispecialty Healthcare Partner

A R Hospital is a premier multispecialty hospital located in Sharadadevi Nagar, Mysuru, Karnataka. We are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services with compassion, ethics, and excellence. With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of experienced specialist doctors across 14 departments, we are committed to delivering the highest standard of medical care to our patients.

Our hospital offers comprehensive healthcare services ranging from routine checkups to advanced surgical procedures. We believe in patient-centric care with a focus on advanced medical technology, treating every patient with dignity, respect, and personalized attention.
